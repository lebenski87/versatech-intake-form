Preparing Your Documents

A successful application depends on complete and accurate documentation. This section provides an interactive checklist of the documents you will need to provide. Click on each category to see the specific items required. This organized approach makes the process less overwhelming.

  • Valid Passport: Must be valid for at least six months beyond your program end date.
  • Birth Certificate: For identity verification.
  • Form I-20: Certificate of Eligibility from your SEVP-certified school.
  • SEVIS I-901 Fee Receipt: Proof of payment for the student tracking system.
  • DS-160 Confirmation Page: From your online visa application.
  • Visa Application Photo: Must meet U.S. visa photo requirements.
  • Transcripts & Diplomas: From all previously attended schools.
  • Standardized Test Scores: TOEFL, IELTS, SAT, GRE, GMAT, as required by your institution.
  • Other Certificates: Any additional relevant academic or professional certifications.
  • Bank Statements: Proof of sufficient funds to cover all study and living expenses.
  • Sponsor Letter / Affidavit of Support: If applicable (e.g., Form I-134).
  • Proof of Ties to Home Country: Critical for demonstrating intent to return after studies (e.g., property deeds, job offers, family business info).
  • Personal Statement: A narrative outlining your academic goals and future plans.
